LAMBORGHINI MIURA FEELS AT HOME ON THE SNOW

The Lamborghini Miura has always had a unique relationship with the snow, ever since the Shah of Persia asked to take delivery of his new Miura in the Sixties at his chalet in St. Moritz so he could have fun driving it on the snow. Over the years, many owners have driven it also in the winter and – at least the bravest of them – experienced the thrill of controlling the most powerful 12-cylinder engine of its day on the snow.
